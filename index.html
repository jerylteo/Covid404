Covid 404 proudly presents an automated entry to anywhere with an entrance - Malls, Buildings, we've got it covered for you so that you won't have to worry about your safety and a long queue any longer.

FREE FOR ALL

Covid 404 is a non-profit organization and its only aim is to provide assistance and reassurance to everyone in this chaotic period of the Covid-19 outbreak.

The Automatic System

Security

A priority of the automated entry process is security. With several sensors in place, you can be rest assured that everyone in the building is safe and free from any risk.

Non-existent Queues

With the fast, automated system in place, everyone will be able to enter buildings faster than ever.

No Manpower

With a fully automated process, no manpower is required at all, saving owners the unnecessary expenditure to hire more people.
